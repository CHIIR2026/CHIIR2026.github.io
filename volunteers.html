CALL FOR VOLUNTEERS

Call for Student Volunteers

Thank you for your interest in serving as a Student Volunteer (SV) for CHIIR 2026!

Student volunteers play a crucial role in ensuring that the conference runs smoothly and that attendees have an excellent experience. As an SV, your registration fee will be fully covered, and you will gain an opportunity to see how a major international research conference operates behind the scenes.

This role offers a meaningful chance to connect with researchers, practitioners, and fellow students from around the world, while contributing directly to the success of the CHIIR community. The information collected in this form will help us with the selection process, scheduling, and communication throughout the preparation period and the conference itself.

Last date to apply: December 31, 2025
