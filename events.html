SPECIAL EVENTS

Special Events

Join us for three evening social events that showcase the University of Washington campus and Seattle’s unique culture.

Welcome Reception at the Burke Museum

Date & time: Sunday, March 22, 2026, 6:00–8:00 PM (PT)

Location: Burke Museum of Natural History and Culture, University of Washington

Start CHIIR 2026 with an evening at the Burke Museum, Washington's oldest public museum and a hub for natural history and culture. Conference attendees will have access to all public galleries.

The reception starts at 6:30pm and the registration desk will be open from 6pm. Please make sure to stop by at the registration deskfirst to pick up your badge and drink tickets. Note that you may be asked for your ID (e.g., a valid driver's license, a passport, etc.).

Burke Museum website  |  View on Google Maps

Monday Night Bowling Social (HUB Games)

Date & time: Monday, March 23, 2026, 5:00–7:00 PM (PT)

Location: HUB Games Bowling Alley, Husky Union Building (HUB)

Right after the Industry Panel, we head to the HUB Bowling Alley for an informal evening of bowling and pizza at HUB Games.

HUB Games bowling information  |  View on Google Maps

"The Boys in the Boat" Tour & Social Dinner at Conibear Shellhouse

Date & time (dinner): Tuesday, March 24, 2026, 6:00–9:00 PM (PT)

Location: Conibear Shellhouse, University of Washington

Our main conference dinner will be held at Conibear Shellhouse, home of the storied Washington Rowing. The venue sits on the water and is within walking distance from the HUB, offering a memorable setting to close the day.

We are excited to invite CHIIR 2026 full conference attendees to a "Boys in the Boat" tour at Conibear Shellhouse right before dinner, from 4:30–6:00 PM (PT).

The tour typically costs $30 per person, but this cost will be covered by the conference. There is no additional charge to attend. Due to limited capacity, you must sign up for one of three 30-minute slots.

Please sign up by Friday, March 20th to reserve your spot: Conibear Tour Sign-up.
